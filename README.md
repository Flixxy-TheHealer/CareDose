# 💊 CareDose

**CareDose** is a Flutter-based healthcare companion app designed to help senior citizens and patients manage their daily medicines efficiently. The app provides medicine reminders, dose tracking, inventory management, and low-stock alerts to improve medication adherence and reduce missed doses.

---

## 📖 Problem Statement

Many elderly people and patients with chronic illnesses take multiple medicines every day. Remembering medication schedules can be difficult, leading to:

- Missed doses
- Double consumption of medicines
- Running out of medicines unexpectedly
- Poor treatment adherence

CareDose aims to solve these problems through a simple, accessible, and user-friendly mobile application.

---

## ✨ Features

### 💊 Daily Dose Tracking
- Mark medicines as taken with a single tap.
- View daily medication history.
- Easy-to-use interface for senior citizens.

### ⏰ Medicine Reminders
- Timely notifications for scheduled medicines.
- Helps users maintain medication discipline.

### ⚠️ Missed Dose Alerts
- Detects when a scheduled dose is skipped.
- Sends reminders to prevent missed medication.

### 📦 Medicine Inventory Management
- Track available medicine stock.
- Automatically update inventory after each dose.

### 🔔 Low Stock Reminder
- Alerts users before medicine stock runs low.
- Prevents treatment interruptions.

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Follow-up Tracking
- Store upcoming doctor appointments.
- Keep treatment schedules organized.
